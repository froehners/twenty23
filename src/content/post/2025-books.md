I read **86** books this year, 22 books less than last year, but still a decent number! I also DNF'ed quite a few, it was very freeing! 


# Here are some standouts:

1. Four Thousand Weeks: Time Management for Mortals, by Oliver Burkeman
	*I really loved this quote: "Choose uncomfortable enlargement over comfortable diminishment whenever you can."*
2. Art & Fear: Observations on the Perils (and Rewards) of Artmaking, by David Bayles and Ted Orland
	*I bought this book in college, and it's been through many moves. Extremely gratifying to have read this finally!*
3. Heated Rivalry (Game Changers #2), by Rachel Reid 
	*Yes, I'm obsessed with the TV show. Yes, the book lived up to the hype.*
4. Prequel: An American Fight Against Fascism, by Rachel Maddow
	*Fingers crossed we do that again????*
5. Devils Inc., by Lauren Palphreyman
	*Honestly just loved the concept of accidentally selling your soul for free wifi.*
