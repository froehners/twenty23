Dinophilia is a concept band that describes themselves as "an all-out punk-rock, sludge-metal, overly-hyphenated, genre-bending, mind-melting band that performs songs about dinosaurs, for dinosaurs, by dinosaurs" (and yes, it is a joke).

![Front and back EP artwork](src/content/post/fun/ep.png)
Artwork and production of the EP, "Mouth Full of Human Head," printed in a short run on recycled cardboard.

![Folded CD sleeve](src/content/post/fun/demo.png)
Demo concept - the cd package unfolds into a poster. 

![4 pins](src/content/post/fun/pins.png)
Designing and pressing one-inch pins.

Two gig posters, one inspired by Land of the Lost, and one in collaboration with illustrator Alanna Wynn.
